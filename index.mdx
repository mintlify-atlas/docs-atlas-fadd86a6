## Building Connections Without Barriers

DigiForum is a digital community platform designed to promote social inclusion, emotional support, and meaningful interaction among people with physical disabilities. Our platform integrates accessibility-first design, user-centered principles, and modern web development practices.

## Why DigiForum?

DigiForum was created to provide a digital space where people with physical disabilities can:

- **Share experiences** and personal stories in a supportive environment
- **Seek guidance** from others who understand similar challenges
- **Build connections** with community members worldwide
- **Access resources** about prosthetics, therapy, and daily living
- **Navigate safely** with comprehensive accessibility features

<Note>
  DigiForum is built with accessibility at its core, featuring adjustable text sizes, high contrast modes, screen reader support, and more.
</Note>
