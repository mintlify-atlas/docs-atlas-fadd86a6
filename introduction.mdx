## What is DigiForum?

DigiForum is a **digital community platform** designed specifically for people with amputations to share experiences, seek guidance, and strengthen their skills while navigating the digital world safely and inclusively.

<Info>
**Mission**: To build a safe, empathetic space where every story deserves to be heard, and every voice matters in our community.
</Info>

## Who is DigiForum For?

DigiForum is designed for:

## Key Benefits

### Mutual Support
A empathetic community where each experience adds value and every voice matters. Share your story, ask questions, and receive support from people who truly understand.

### Real Connection
Connect with people who genuinely comprehend your situation and challenges. Build meaningful relationships based on shared experiences.

### Resource Exchange
Access information, advice, and resources shared by the community, including:
- Prosthetic technology information
- Rehabilitation tips and exercises
- Emotional support strategies
- Practical daily living advice

## Platform Features

### Community Forum
Create and browse posts in six dedicated categories:

- **Personal Experiences**: Share your journey and milestones
- **Questions & Answers**: Ask the community for advice and information
- **Useful Resources**: Share helpful tools, websites, apps, and services
- **Emotional Support**: Find and offer encouragement during difficult times
- **Technology & Prosthetics**: Discuss advances and experiences with prosthetic devices
- **Other**: Everything else that doesn't fit the above categories

### User Profiles
Manage your personal information including:
- Full name and username
- Profile photo
- Amputation type (optional)
- Date of birth (optional)
- View your post history
- Access saved posts

### Post Interactions
- **Like Posts**: Show appreciation with heart reactions
- **Save Posts**: Bookmark important posts for later reference
- **Comment**: Engage in conversations and provide support
- **Search & Filter**: Find relevant content by category, author, or keywords
