## Engaging with the Community

DigiForum is built on meaningful connections and interactions. Learn how to engage with posts through likes, comments, saved posts, and search features.

## Finding Posts

### Browsing the Dashboard

The Dashboard (Foro de la Comunidad) displays all community posts in reverse chronological order, with the newest posts appearing first.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Click **"Foro"** or **"Dashboard"** in the main navigation menu to view all community posts.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Posts">
    Scroll through the feed to see:
    - Post titles and categories (color-coded badges)
    - Author names and publication dates
    - Content previews (first few lines)
    - Like counts and save status
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Searching Posts

Find specific content using the search feature:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Search Bar">
    At the top of the Dashboard, enter keywords in the search field labeled **"Buscar por título, contenido, categoría o autor..."** (Search by title, content, category, or author...).
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Search Results">
    As you type, the post list automatically filters to show only matching posts. The search looks through:
    - Post titles
    - Post content
    - Category names
    - Author names
  </Step>

  <Step title="Clear Search">
    Delete your search query to return to viewing all posts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filtering by Category

Narrow down posts by specific topics:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Category Filter">
    Below the search bar, you'll see category filter buttons:
    - **Todas** (All): Show all posts
    - **Experiencias personales** (Purple)
    - **Preguntas y respuestas** (Blue)
    - **Recursos útiles** (Green)
    - **Apoyo emocional** (Pink)
    - **Tecnología y prótesis** (Orange)
    - **Otros** (Gray)
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Filtered Results">
    Click any category button to see only posts in that category. The selected category button will be highlighted.
    
    The heading will update to show: "Resultados - [Category Name] (X)" where X is the number of posts in that category.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Combine with Search">
    You can use category filters and search together to narrow your results even further.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Category filters and search work together seamlessly. Filter by category, then use search to find specific posts within that category.
</Info>

## Reading Posts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on a Post">
    From the Dashboard, click the **"Leer más"** (Read more) button on any post card to view the full post.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Complete Content">
    The post detail page displays:
    - Category badge
    - Full title
    - Author name
    - Publication date and time
    - Complete post content
    - All comments
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to Dashboard">
    Click **"Volver al Foro"** (Return to Forum) at the top of the page to go back to the post feed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Liking Posts

Show appreciation for posts by liking them:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Like Button">
    On each post card in the Dashboard, look for the heart emoji button in the bottom-left corner.
    
    - **White heart (🤍)**: You haven't liked this post yet
    - **Red heart (❤️)**: You've already liked this post
  </Step>

  <Step title="Like or Unlike">
    Click the heart button to:
    - **Add your like**: If you haven't liked the post yet
    - **Remove your like**: If you've already liked it
    
    The number next to the heart shows the total like count for that post.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Likes are a simple way to show support and appreciation. They help highlight valuable content in the community.
</Info>

## Saving Posts

Bookmark posts for easy access later:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the Save Button">
    On each post card, click the pin emoji button (📌) next to the like button.
    
    - **"📌 Guardar"** (Save): Post is not saved yet
    - **"📌 Guardado"** (Saved): Post is saved to your collection
  </Step>

  <Step title="Toggle Save Status">
    Click the button to:
    - **Save the post**: Add it to your saved collection
    - **Unsave the post**: Remove it from your collection
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Saved Posts">
    View all your saved posts from your profile:
    1. Go to **"Mi Perfil"** (My Profile)
    2. Click the **"Guardadas"** (Saved) tab
    3. Browse your saved posts and click **"Ver"** (View) to read them
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Save posts that contain useful resources, important information, or content you want to revisit later. Your saved posts are private and only visible to you.
</Tip>

## Commenting on Posts

Join the conversation by adding comments:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a Post">
    Click **"Leer más"** (Read more) on any post to view its detail page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scroll to Comments Section">
    Below the post content, you'll see the **"Comentarios"** (Comments) section showing the number of existing comments.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Comment">
    In the text area labeled **"Agregar un comentario"** (Add a comment):
    - Type your response, question, or feedback
    - Keep it respectful and relevant
    - Use multiple lines if needed for clarity
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Your Comment">
    Click the **"Comentar"** (Comment) button with the send icon to post your comment.
    
    Your comment will appear immediately in the comments list, showing:
    - Your name
    - The date and time of posting
    - Your comment text
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Comments

All comments on a post are displayed in chronological order:

- **Author name** appears in bold
- **Date and time** show when the comment was posted
- **Comment content** appears below the metadata

Comments have a colored left border for visual separation.

<Warning>
  Comments cannot be edited or deleted after posting. Make sure your comment is complete and appropriate before submitting.
</Warning>

## Comment Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep Comments Constructive">
    Whether agreeing or disagreeing, maintain a constructive and helpful tone. Focus on the topic, not personal attacks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Share Your Experience">
    Personal experiences in comments help others learn and feel less alone. Don't hesitate to share relevant stories.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ask Clarifying Questions">
    If you need more information from the post author, ask respectful questions to better understand their situation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Offer Support">
    A simple word of encouragement can mean a lot. If a post resonates with you, let the author know.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Respect Privacy">
    Don't ask for or share personal contact information in public comments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Think Before Posting">
    Since comments can't be edited, take a moment to review before clicking "Comentar" (Comment).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing Your Interactions

### View Your Activity

Track your community participation through your profile:

1. **Your Posts**: See all posts you've created in the "Mis Publicaciones" (My Posts) tab
2. **Saved Posts**: Access your bookmarked posts in the "Guardadas" (Saved) tab
3. **Activity Stats**: View your post count and saved post count on your profile

<Info>
  There's currently no dedicated page to view your comments or likes. These appear on the posts themselves.
</Info>

## Search and Discovery Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Specific Keywords">
    When searching, use specific words related to what you're looking for. For example, search "fisioterapeuta" (physiotherapist) instead of just "ayuda" (help).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Try Different Search Terms">
    If you don't find what you're looking for, try synonyms or related terms.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Browse by Category First">
    If you're looking for a specific type of content, filter by category first, then use search within that category.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check Regularly">
    New posts appear at the top of the feed. Visit the Dashboard regularly to see the latest community content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Save Important Posts">
    When you find valuable information, save the post so you can easily find it again later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Interaction Tasks

<Steps>
  <Step title="Finding Posts About Prosthetics">
    1. Filter by "Tecnología y prótesis" (orange category)
    2. Or search for keywords like "prótesis", "biónica", "tecnología"
  </Step>

    1. Filter by "Apoyo emocional" (pink category)
    2. Read posts from others sharing similar experiences
    3. Leave supportive comments or create your own post
  </Step>

    1. Filter by "Recursos útiles" (green category)
    2. Save helpful posts for future reference
    3. Consider sharing your own resources
  </Step>

  <Step title="Asking Questions">
    1. Search to see if your question has been asked before
    2. If not, create a new post in "Preguntas y respuestas" (blue category)
    3. Check back for comments with answers and advice
  </Step>
</Steps>
