## Creating a New Post

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Navigate to the **Dashboard** (Foro de la Comunidad) from the main navigation menu. This is where all community posts are displayed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the Create Post Form">
    Scroll to the **"Crear Nueva Publicación"** (Create New Post) section at the top of the Dashboard and click the **"Comenzar"** (Begin) button.
    
    The post creation form will expand, showing three required fields.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Post Title">
    Enter a clear, descriptive title in the **"Título"** field:
    
    - Keep it concise but informative
    - Accurately reflect your post's content
    - Make it easy for others to understand what your post is about
    
    **Good examples:**
    - "Mi primer día con prótesis" (My first day with a prosthetic)
    - "¿Recomendaciones de fisioterapeutas especializados?" (Specialized physiotherapist recommendations?)
    - "Recursos gratuitos online para ejercicios en casa" (Free online resources for home exercises)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Category">
    Choose the most appropriate category from the **"Categoría"** dropdown menu:
    
    - **Experiencias personales** (Personal experiences): Share your personal journey, milestones, and daily life experiences
    - **Preguntas y respuestas** (Questions and answers): Ask questions or seek advice from the community
    - **Recursos útiles** (Useful resources): Share helpful links, tools, apps, or information
    - **Apoyo emocional** (Emotional support): Seek or provide emotional support and encouragement
    - **Tecnología y prótesis** (Technology and prosthetics): Discuss prosthetic technology, advances, and experiences
    - **Otros** (Other): For content that doesn't fit the above categories
    
    <Info>
      Selecting the right category helps community members find posts relevant to their interests and needs.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Content">
    In the **"Contenido"** (Content) text area, write your post:
    
    - Be clear and detailed
    - Use paragraph breaks for readability
    - Share relevant context and information
    - Be authentic and genuine
    - Maintain a respectful tone
    
    <Tip>
      The content field supports multiple paragraphs and preserves your formatting. Take your time to organize your thoughts clearly.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Your Post">
    Before publishing, review:
    
    - Title is clear and accurate
    - Category is appropriate
    - Content is complete and free of errors
    - Tone is respectful and appropriate
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish Your Post">
    Click the **"Publicar"** (Publish) button to share your post with the community.
    
    You'll see a success message, and your post will immediately appear in the community feed. The form will reset, ready for your next post.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Post Categories Explained

Each category has a specific purpose and color-coded badge for easy identification:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Experiencias personales (Purple)" icon="user">
    Share personal stories, achievements, challenges, and day-to-day experiences. This is where you can talk about your journey, rehabilitation progress, adapting to life changes, and personal milestones.
    
    **Example topics:**
    - First experiences with prosthetics
    - Rehabilitation progress updates
    - Overcoming daily challenges
    - Personal achievements and victories
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Preguntas y respuestas (Blue)" icon="question">
    Ask questions and seek advice from community members. Whether you need recommendations, have technical questions, or want opinions, this is the place to ask.
    
    **Example topics:**
    - Seeking healthcare provider recommendations
    - Questions about prosthetic options
    - Advice on rehabilitation techniques
    - General inquiries about community resources
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Recursos útiles (Green)" icon="book">
    Share helpful resources such as websites, apps, videos, articles, books, or tools that have helped you. This is a collaborative resource library for the community.
    
    **Example topics:**
    - Exercise videos or apps
    - Helpful websites or online communities
    - Books or educational materials
    - Local services or programs
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Apoyo emocional (Pink)" icon="heart">
    Seek or provide emotional support, encouragement, and understanding. This is a safe space to discuss feelings, struggles, and emotional well-being.
    
    **Example topics:**
    - Coping with emotional challenges
    - Seeking encouragement during difficult times
    - Sharing how you've overcome mental health struggles
    - Offering support to others
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tecnología y prótesis (Orange)" icon="microchip">
    Discuss prosthetic technology, innovations, experiences with different devices, and technological advances in the field.
    
    **Example topics:**
    - Reviews of prosthetic devices
    - New technological developments
    - Comparisons of different prosthetic options
    - Technical questions about devices
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Otros (Gray)" icon="ellipsis">
    For content that doesn't fit into the other categories. Use this sparingly and consider if your post might better fit one of the specific categories.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Formatting Your Content

While the post editor doesn't support rich text formatting (bold, italics, etc.), you can still make your posts readable:

- Use **line breaks** to separate paragraphs
- Keep **paragraphs short** for easy reading
- Use **clear, simple language**
- **Structure your thoughts** logically
- Start with context, then details

## Canceling a Post

If you change your mind while creating a post:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Cancel Button">
    Click **"Cancelar"** (Cancel) at the bottom of the post form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Form Will Reset">
    All your entered information will be cleared, and the form will collapse back to its initial state. You can start fresh whenever you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  There's no "save as draft" feature. If you cancel, all your entered information will be lost. Consider writing longer posts in a text editor first, then copying them into the form.
</Warning>

## After Publishing

Once your post is published:

- It appears immediately in the community feed
- Other members can read, like, comment, and save it
- You can view it in your profile under "Mis Publicaciones" (My Posts)
- The post cannot be edited or deleted (contact an administrator if needed)

<Info>
  Posts are currently permanent and cannot be edited after publishing. Make sure to review carefully before clicking "Publicar" (Publish).
</Info>

## Tips for Engaging Posts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be Authentic">
    Share genuine experiences and feelings. The community values honesty and authenticity.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Provide Context">
    Give enough background information so others can understand your situation or question.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Be Specific">
    Specific questions or stories are easier for others to engage with than vague posts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Encourage Discussion">
    Ask questions or invite others to share their experiences to foster engagement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Show Empathy">
    Remember that everyone in the community is on their own journey. Be supportive and understanding.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
