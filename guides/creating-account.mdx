## Welcome to DigiForum

Joining DigiForum is quick and easy. This guide will walk you through creating your account and setting up your profile.

## Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Registration Page">
    Visit the DigiForum homepage and click the **"Crear Cuenta"** (Create Account) button, or go directly to the authentication page and select the **"Crear Cuenta"** tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in Required Information">
    Complete all required fields marked with an asterisk (*):
    
    - **Full Name** (Nombre Completo): Enter your complete name
    - **Email** (Correo Electrónico): Provide a valid email address
    - **Username** (Nombre de Usuario): Choose a unique username
    - **Password** (Contraseña): Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
    - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm
    
    <Warning>
      Your password must be at least 8 characters long. Make sure both password fields match exactly.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Optional Profile Information">
    While optional, this information helps us provide a better community experience:
    
    - **Date of Birth** (Fecha de Nacimiento): Select your birth date using the calendar picker
    - **Amputation Type** (Tipo de Amputación): Choose from:
      - Brazo (Arm)
      - Mano (Hand)
      - Pierna (Leg)
      - Pie (Foot)
      - Múltiples (Multiple)
      - Prefiero no decir (Prefer not to say)
    
    <Info>
      This information is entirely optional and can be updated later in your profile settings.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Your Registration">
    Review your information and click the **"Crear Cuenta"** (Create Account) button at the bottom of the form.
    
    You'll see a success message confirming your account has been created, and you'll be automatically redirected to the login page after 2 seconds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In to Your New Account">
    Use your email address or username along with your password to log in and start participating in the community.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Registration Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Email Already Exists">
    If you see the error "Ya existe una cuenta con este correo electrónico" (An account already exists with this email), you may have already registered. Try logging in instead, or use a different email address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Username Already Taken">
    If you receive "Este nombre de usuario ya está en uso" (This username is already in use), choose a different username. Usernames must be unique across the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invalid Email Format">
    Make sure your email address is in the correct format (e.g., usuario@ejemplo.com). The system validates email addresses during registration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password Requirements">
    Your password must be at least 8 characters long. If you receive an error about password length, make sure you're meeting this minimum requirement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Passwords Don't Match">
    If you see "Las contraseñas no coinciden" (Passwords don't match), double-check that you've entered the exact same password in both the Password and Confirm Password fields.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What Happens After Registration?

Once you've successfully created your account:

1. **Automatic Login**: After your first login, you'll be directed to the Dashboard where you can see all community posts
2. **Profile Access**: You can view and edit your profile at any time from the navigation menu
3. **Community Participation**: You're ready to start creating posts, commenting, and engaging with the community
