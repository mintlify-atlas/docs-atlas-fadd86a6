## Your Profile Overview

Your profile is your personal space in the DigiForum community. You can view your information, update your details, change your password, and track your activity.

## Accessing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Your Profile">
    Click on **"Mi Perfil"** (My Profile) in the navigation menu at the top of any page when logged in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Your Profile Information">
    Your profile displays:
    - Profile photo (if uploaded)
    - Full name and username
    - Email address
    - Age (calculated from date of birth, if provided)
    - Amputation type
    - Member since date
    - Number of posts created
    - Number of saved posts
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Edit Mode">
    Click the **"Editar"** (Edit) button in the top-right corner of your profile card.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Your Profile Photo">
    Upload a new profile photo by selecting an image file:
    
    - **Supported formats**: JPG, PNG, WEBP
    - **Maximum size**: 5MB
    - **Preview**: You'll see a preview of your photo before saving
    
    <Info>
      Choose a clear, appropriate photo that represents you. Profile photos help personalize the community experience.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update Personal Information">
    Modify any of the following fields:
    
    - **Full Name** (Nombre Completo): Update your display name
    - **Date of Birth** (Fecha de Nacimiento): Change using the calendar picker
    - **Amputation Type** (Tipo de Amputación): Select a different type or choose "No especificar" (Not specified)
    
    <Note>
      Your email address and username cannot be changed after registration for security reasons.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Your Changes">
    Click the **"Guardar"** (Save) button to apply your updates, or click the **X** button to cancel without saving.
    
    You'll see a confirmation message when your profile has been updated successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing Your Password

For security, you can change your password at any time from the profile edit screen.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Password Change Section">
    While in edit mode, scroll down to the **"Cambiar Contraseña"** (Change Password) section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Password Information">
    Fill in the following fields:
    
    - **Current Password** (Contraseña Actual): Your existing password
    - **New Password** (Nueva Contraseña): Your new password (minimum 6 characters)
    - **Confirm New Password** (Confirmar Nueva Contraseña): Re-enter your new password
    
    <Warning>
      You must fill in all three password fields to change your password. The system will verify your current password before allowing the change.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Password Changes">
    Click **"Guardar"** (Save) to update your password. You'll receive a confirmation message when the change is successful.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Requirements

- Minimum length: 6 characters
- Must match in both "New Password" and "Confirm New Password" fields
- Your current password must be correct

## Viewing Your Activity

Your profile page includes two tabs to track your community participation:

### My Posts Tab

View all posts you've created:

- See post titles, categories, and creation dates
- Preview post content
- Click **"Ver"** (View) to open any post
- Track your total number of published posts

<Info>
  If you haven't created any posts yet, you'll see a message encouraging you to share your first story with a link to the forum.
</Info>

### Saved Posts Tab

Access posts you've saved for later:

- Review posts you've bookmarked
- Easily find important resources or discussions
- Click **"Ver"** (View) to revisit any saved post

## Privacy Settings

While editing your profile, you have control over what information you share:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Amputation Type Privacy">
    You can choose "Prefiero no decir" (Prefer not to say) or "No especificar" (Not specified) if you don't want to share this information with the community.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Date of Birth Privacy">
    Your exact date of birth is never displayed publicly. Only your calculated age (if you choose to provide your birth date) appears on your profile.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email Privacy">
    Your email address is visible on your profile page to you only. Other community members cannot see your email address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Profile Photo">
    Profile photos are optional. You can choose not to upload a photo, and a default icon will be displayed instead.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Profile Management Tasks

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I remove my profile photo?">
    Currently, you can only replace your profile photo with a new one. To remove it entirely, you would need to contact an administrator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my username?">
    No, usernames cannot be changed after registration to maintain consistency across posts and comments. Choose your username carefully during registration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I forget my current password?">
    If you've forgotten your password while trying to change it, you'll need to contact the administrator for password recovery assistance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete my account?">
    Account deletion is not currently available through the profile interface. Please contact an administrator if you wish to delete your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can't I change my email address?">
    Email addresses are linked to your account for security and cannot be changed. This prevents unauthorized account modifications.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
